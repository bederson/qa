Keshif means discovery and exploration in Turkish (Original: ke≈üif).

Simply, it is designed for YOU and your data.

  • Left: categories. Top: time. All: in synch.
  • Mix and match your search. "And" selection across different filters, "or" inside same filter.
  • Text search: All items in the collections, or categories of a filtering.
  • Time filtering: Drag & drop handles, set your range.
Keshif project is open source on github! Get your fork, serve yourself.
